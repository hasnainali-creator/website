# Accessibility Statement

OmnySports is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## 1. Standard Compliance
We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide array of disabilities.

## 2. Features
Our website includes features such as:
*   Clear font hierarchy for readability.
*   High contrast ratios for text and background.
*   Keyboard-friendly navigation.
*   Alt-text for images to assist screen readers.

## 3. Feedback
We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of OmnySports. If you encounter accessibility barriers on our site, please let us know so we can address them.
