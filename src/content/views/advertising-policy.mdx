# ADVERTISING POLICY

**Effective Date: \[Insert Date]**

This Advertising Policy defines how monetization operates on OmnySports, how advertising relationships are managed, and how transparency is maintained between the platform, its readers, and commercial partners.

OmnySports is committed to maintaining editorial integrity while operating a sustainable publishing model. Advertising is a revenue mechanism — not an editorial influence.

## These documents collectively define the operational and legal framework of the platform.

# 1. Overview of Advertising Structure

OmnySports currently displays advertisements through Raptive, a professional ad management network.

These advertisements may include:

* Display banner ads
* In-content advertisements
* Programmatic ads
* Video ads
* Sponsored display placements

Ad placements are automatically managed through advertising systems and may vary depending on user location, device type, and browsing behavior.

Advertising is part of maintaining the sustainability of the platform and supporting continued sports journalism coverage.

---

# 2. Editorial Independence from Advertising

Advertising does not influence:

* Match coverage
* Tournament analysis
* Player evaluation
* Transfer reporting
* Statistical interpretation
* Article conclusions

Editorial decisions are governed exclusively by the standards described in our\
**Editorial Policy** → (Link this to /editorial-policy/)

Advertisers cannot:

* Demand positive coverage
* Suppress unfavorable reporting
* Alter published analysis
* Pre-approve independent news coverage

OmnySports retains full editorial control over all published material.

This separation ensures credibility and protects reader trust.

---

# 3. Sponsored Content Policy

OmnySports may accept sponsored content under controlled conditions.

However, strict restrictions apply.

Sponsored content must:

* Be relevant to sports audiences
* Align with platform values
* Avoid misleading claims
* Comply with applicable advertising laws
* Be factually verifiable

Before approval, sponsored material is reviewed for:

* Authenticity
* Brand legitimacy
* User safety
* Legal compliance

If a post is sponsored, it will be clearly labeled as:

“Sponsored”\
or\
“Paid Partnership”

Transparency is mandatory.

Sponsored labeling will not be hidden or disguised.

However, sponsorship does not guarantee endorsement beyond the disclosed partnership agreement.

---

# 4. Restrictions on Sponsored Content

OmnySports will not accept sponsored material that promotes:

* Illegal activities
* Gambling operations without proper regulation
* Adult content
* Fraudulent schemes
* Financial scams
* Unverified medical products
* Political propaganda
* Hate speech

Even if compensation is offered, such content will be rejected.

User protection takes priority over revenue.

Further usage limitations and platform conduct expectations are described in our\
**Terms of Service** → (Link this to /terms-of-service/)

Affiliate inclusion does not constitute product endorsement beyond informational discussion.

* Security systems

# 13. Sponsored Content Review and Approval Process

Before sponsored material is published on OmnySports, it undergoes internal review.

* Brand legitimacy
* Regulatory compliance
