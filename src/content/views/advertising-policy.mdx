# Advertising Policy&#x20;

## Commercial Relationships Built on Transparency. Editorial Independence That Is Never for Sale.

---

There is a question every serious reader should ask of any news platform they trust:

*Who is paying for this — and what are they getting in return?*

It is a fair question. In fact, it is an essential one. The relationship between advertising revenue and editorial content has quietly corrupted more media organisations than any single journalistic failure ever could. When advertisers start influencing newsrooms, the reader always loses — and they rarely even know it is happening.

At OmnySports, we answer that question openly, completely, and without hesitation.

Yes, we carry advertising. It funds the infrastructure, the editorial team, and the real-time technology that delivers the journalism you read every single day. But what our advertisers purchase is precisely and formally defined — and what they do not get is even clearer.

**They do not get our editorial independence. Not for any price.**

As we have always maintained — and as deeply outlined in our [About Us](/about) page — OmnySports was built because the modern sports fan deserves better than a newsroom that is quietly for sale. This Advertising Policy is the commercial expression of that founding commitment.

---

## Our Core Advertising Principle

Every commercial decision at OmnySports is governed by one non-negotiable rule:

**Advertising revenue never influences editorial content.**

This is not an aspiration. It is an operational reality enforced through formal structural separation between our commercial team and our newsroom. The two departments do not share decision-making authority. They do not review each other's work before publication. They do not negotiate on behalf of each other — ever.

When our journalists write a match report, a transfer analysis, or an investigative piece — no advertiser has seen it, shaped it, or approved it. When our editors publish a critical opinion column about a governing body that happens to be a commercial partner, that column runs without interference.

This separation is the foundation of everything we do. Without it, nothing else we claim about our journalism means anything. The editorial standards that protect this independence are published in full in our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy) — a document we encourage every reader to study carefully.

## What Advertising on OmnySports Looks Like

### Standard Display Advertising

OmnySports carries standard digital display advertising — banner ads, sidebar placements, and in-article ad units served through verified advertising networks. These are clearly positioned as advertisements and visually distinct from editorial content at all times.

**Advertisers who purchase display placements receive:**

* Agreed placement positions on specified page types
* Defined audience reach metrics
* Campaign performance reporting

**Advertisers who purchase display placements do not receive:**

* Any form of editorial access or input
* Advance sight of content appearing alongside their ads
* The ability to request removal or amendment of editorial content
* Any guaranteed association with specific stories or topics

---

### Sponsored & Branded Content

Occasionally, OmnySports produces sponsored content in partnership with commercial partners. This content is created by our team to an agreed brief and published on our platform.

Every piece of sponsored content on OmnySports is subject to the following mandatory standards:

* It is **always** labelled clearly and prominently as *"Sponsored Content"* or *"Paid Partnership"* — before the reader engages with it
* It is **never** presented in a format designed to be mistaken for independent editorial journalism
* It must meet our factual accuracy standards — we do not publish sponsored content that is misleading, even if a commercial partner requests it
* The commercial relationship is **always** fully disclosed

If you are ever unsure whether a piece of content on OmnySports is editorially independent or commercially produced, the label will tell you. Unambiguously. Every time. For further context on how we formally distinguish between reporting, analysis, and commercial content, our [Disclaimer](/disclaimer) page provides a complete breakdown.

---

### Affiliate & Performance-Based Content

Where OmnySports content contains affiliate links — links through which we may earn a commission if you make a purchase — this is disclosed clearly within the relevant article or page.

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial recommendations. If we recommend a product, service, or platform, it is because our editorial team considers it genuinely valuable to our readers — not because an affiliate commission exists.

Our journalism is never constructed around affiliate opportunities. Affiliate disclosures are applied where relevant, not used as the reason a piece of content is created.

## Who We Accept Advertising From

OmnySports maintains strict advertiser standards. Not every commercial partner is accepted, and not every category of advertising is permitted on our platform.

### Advertisers We Welcome

* Sports equipment and apparel brands
* Broadcast and streaming platforms carrying legitimate sports rights
* Sports nutrition and performance brands with verified, responsible marketing practices
* Travel and hospitality brands relevant to sporting events and fan experiences
* Technology and media brands aligned with our audience's interests
* Educational and professional development platforms

### Advertising Categories We Do Not Accept

The following categories are **prohibited on OmnySports** — regardless of commercial value:

* **Unlicensed or unregulated gambling operators** — We do not carry advertising from gambling platforms that are not fully licensed in the jurisdictions where they operate
* **Tobacco, vaping, and nicotine products** — No exceptions
* **Content targeting minors** in any commercially exploitative way
* **Political advertising of any kind** — We carry no paid political content. Our editorial independence extends to full political neutrality in our commercial relationships
* **Misleading health or performance claims** — Advertising making unsubstantiated claims about health, fitness, or sporting outcomes
* **Content conflicting with our community standards** — Any advertising that is discriminatory, offensive, or inconsistent with the values that govern every layer of this platform

If an advertiser's business practices or public conduct fall below the standards we apply to our own journalism, we reserve the right to terminate the commercial relationship immediately — and without apology.

## The Advertising Review Process

Every advertiser and every advertisement that appears on OmnySports goes through a formal review before activation on our platform.

**Step 1 — Initial Application** The prospective advertiser or agency submits a campaign brief and creative assets to our commercial team.

**Step 2 — Category Review** Our commercial team confirms the advertiser falls within our accepted categories and does not appear on our prohibited list.

**Step 3 — Creative Review** Ad creative is reviewed for factual accuracy, legal compliance, and alignment with our platform standards. Misleading creative is rejected at this stage — regardless of commercial value.

**Step 4 — Editorial Firewall Check** Our editorial leadership confirms no conflict of interest exists between the advertiser and any current or planned editorial coverage. This step is non-negotiable and cannot be bypassed.

**Step 5 — Approval & Scheduling** Approved campaigns are scheduled and activated through our ad management system with agreed placement parameters.

**Step 6 — Ongoing Monitoring** Live campaigns are monitored continuously. Any deviation from approved creative or placement terms triggers immediate review.

## The Editorial Firewall

This is the most important section on this page.

The editorial firewall is the structural mechanism that ensures commercial relationships never compromise our journalism. It is not a policy statement — it is a physical and operational separation built into how our organisation functions.

**Our commercial team** manages advertising sales, partnership development, and revenue generation. They know who our commercial partners are.

**Our editorial team** — the journalists, analysts, and editors who produce every article you read — operate independently. They are not informed of which companies are advertising on the platform. They are not asked to soften, avoid, or favour coverage of commercial partners. They are not financially incentivised in any way based on advertiser relationships.

**Our editorial leadership** holds visibility across both sides solely to ensure the firewall holds. Any advertiser that approaches our editorial staff directly seeking to influence content is reported to the editorial board and subject to immediate contract review.

The relentless dedication of our [Editorial Team](/editorial-team) to maintaining this independence is something we consider our most valuable organisational asset. Their standards, their verification processes, and their editorial conduct are governed comprehensively by our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy).

> *"The moment an advertiser believes they have purchased influence over our journalism, they have violated our agreement. We will not hesitate to end that relationship — publicly if necessary."* — **OmnySports Editorial Board**

## Data, Privacy & Advertising

OmnySports does not sell reader data to advertisers. We do not build individual user profiles for advertising targeting purposes. We do not share personally identifiable information with commercial partners under any circumstances.

Display advertising on our platform may involve **contextual targeting** — meaning ads are served based on the general topic of the page you are reading, not based on a personal profile built from your browsing history.

The complete framework governing how we collect, use, and protect your personal data is explained in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy). Our specific use of cookies in connection with advertising delivery — including exactly which cookies are used, what they do, and how to control them — is covered separately in our [Cookie Policy](/cookie-policy). Your preferences are always in your hands.

## Intellectual Property in Advertising

All advertising creative submitted to OmnySports for publication must be the lawful intellectual property of the submitting advertiser or their authorised agency. By submitting creative assets, advertisers confirm they hold all necessary rights and permissions for the content being displayed.

OmnySports accepts no liability for intellectual property infringement arising from advertiser-submitted creative. All such responsibility rests with the submitting party.

For all intellectual property matters related to content published on our platform — including editorial content, not just advertising — our [DMCA](/dmca) framework provides the complete formal process for raising and resolving claims.

## Advertiser Responsibilities

Commercial partnerships on OmnySports are two-way commitments governed formally by our [Terms and Conditions](/terms-and-conditions). We hold ourselves to high standards, and we require our advertising partners to meet the same bar.

By advertising on OmnySports, all partners agree to:

* Provide accurate, truthful, and legally compliant advertising creative
* Disclose all material information relevant to the products or services being promoted
* Comply with all applicable advertising standards, consumer protection laws, and industry codes
* Refrain from any direct or indirect attempt to contact or influence OmnySports editorial staff
* Accept that OmnySports reserves the right to remove any advertisement violating these standards — without prior notice or refund obligation

## Accessibility of Advertising Content

OmnySports is committed to ensuring that advertising content displayed on our platform meets minimum accessibility standards — including adequate colour contrast, screen-reader compatibility for text-based ads, and avoidance of seizure-triggering animation patterns.

Advertisers are encouraged to submit creative that aligns with these standards. Our broader commitment to platform-wide digital accessibility — including our WCAG compliance roadmap — is published on our [Accessibility](/accessibility) page.

## Reader Rights & Reporting Concerns

You have the right to question everything you see on OmnySports. If you encounter advertising that you believe:

* Is misleading or factually inaccurate
* Has been presented in a way that could be confused with independent editorial content
* Promotes a product or service falling into our prohibited categories
* Appears to have influenced our editorial coverage in any way

We want to know immediately. Reach us through our [Contact Us](/contact) page and mark your message clearly as an **"Advertising Concern"**. Every report is reviewed by a senior member of our commercial compliance team within 48 hours.

Reader scrutiny of our commercial practices is not something we tolerate reluctantly. It is something we actively welcome — because it makes us better.

## Changes to This Policy

Our advertising standards evolve alongside our platform, our audience, and the broader digital media landscape. We review this policy regularly and update it when our standards change or when new advertising formats and technologies require formal guidance.

Significant updates will be reflected in a revised "Last Updated" date and communicated through our platform notice system.

Advertising is how quality journalism sustains itself in the modern world. We accept that reality and operate within it — transparently, responsibly, and on our own terms.

**What is for sale:** Audience reach, brand visibility, and association with a trusted global sports journalism platform.

**What is not for sale:** Editorial independence. Journalistic integrity. Reader trust.

Those are not commodities. They are the reason OmnySports exists — and the reason our readers return every day.
