# DMCA & Intellectual Property Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

OmnySports Global Media Group ("OmnySports") respects the intellectual property rights of athletes, organizations, and media creators worldwide. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), we have established the following institutional framework for responding to claims of copyright infringement.

## 1. Compliance Statement
OmnySports honors the statutory requirements of the DMCA. Our editorial team is trained to utilize licensed imagery and fair-use analytical data. However, if you believe your intellectual property has been utilized without authorization on our global network, we provide a formal mechanism for resolution.

## 2. Formal Notification Protocol
To submit a valid takedown request, the copyright owner or an authorized institutional representative must provide our Legal Department with a written communication containing:
*   **Signature:** A physical or electronic signature of the authorized claimant.
*   **Asset Identification:** A detailed description of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
*   **Location Metadata:** The specific URL or technical identifier of the material hosted on OmnySports.
*   **Contact Information:** Official address, telephone number, and institutional email of the claimant.
*   **Good Faith Assertion:** A statement that the claimant has a refined belief that the material is utilized in an unauthorized manner.

## 3. Institutional Counter-Notification
Authors or contributors whose content has been removed may submit a counter-notification if they believe the removal was the result of a misidentification or fair-use miscalculation. OmnySports reserves the right to restore content if a valid counter-notice is provided.
