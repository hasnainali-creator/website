# About OmnySports&#x20;

OmnySports began with a simple belief: sports deserve coverage that is focused, responsible, and built for the long term.

Founded in 2026 and headquartered in New York, United States, OmnySports was created as a dedicated global sports newsroom — not as a multi-topic media outlet chasing traffic across every trending subject. From day one, the intention was clear: concentrate fully on sports and build credibility through consistency.

We do not branch into politics.\
We do not publish celebrity gossip.\
We do not amplify unverified rumors.

## We cover sports — seriously, carefully, and with purpose.

## Why OmnySports Exists

The modern sports media landscape moves at extraordinary speed. Transfer speculation spreads within minutes. Injury rumors circulate before official confirmation. Headlines are often written for reaction rather than clarity.

OmnySports was built to operate differently.

Our purpose is not to be the loudest voice in the room. It is to be a reliable one.

Every story published on this platform follows internal newsroom standards designed to protect clarity, accuracy, and context (is phrase **internal newsroom standards** par apne Editorial Policy page ka link lagao).

We believe sports journalism should inform, not inflame.\
It should clarify, not confuse.

## A Focused Sports-Only Newsroom

OmnySports exists exclusively for sports coverage.

That focus allows us to:

* Develop deeper subject familiarity
* Maintain consistent terminology
* Monitor leagues and tournaments continuously
* Provide structured updates rather than reactive commentary

Our editorial coverage includes professional leagues, international competitions, athlete career developments, management decisions, and structural changes within the sports industry.

Behind this coverage is a team of editors, contributors, and research specialists whose roles are transparently presented (is phrase **team of editors, contributors, and research specialists** par apne Authors page ka link lagao).

People matter in journalism. Accountability begins with knowing who stands behind the work.

## How We Approach Reporting

Responsible reporting is not accidental — it is structured.

Before publication, sports developments are reviewed internally to ensure:

* The information comes from reliable sourcing
* The headline reflects confirmed facts
* The context explains why the development matters
* The language remains neutral and precise

If a story is still developing, we make that clear.\
If information is confirmed, we say so directly.

Our broader editorial framework that guides sourcing, review, and updates is outlined separately (is phrase **editorial framework** par apne Editorial Policy page ka link lagao).

## Editorial Independence

OmnySports operates independently.

We are not owned by a league.\
We are not controlled by a team.\
We are not directed by an athlete’s management.

This independence allows us to maintain objectivity in coverage decisions.

While OmnySports may work with advertising and affiliate partners to sustain operations, editorial decisions remain separate from commercial relationships.
