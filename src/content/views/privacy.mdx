# Privacy Policy – OmnySports

OmnySports respects digital privacy as a core operational principle.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed, monitored, protected, retained, and governed when users access or interact with our platform. It applies to all visitors worldwide, including users located in the United States, the European Union, and other international jurisdictions.

## Our background, mission, and organizational structure are outlined separately (on the phrase **organizational background** add link to About page). This document focuses strictly and exclusively on privacy practices and data governance standards.

# 1. Our Digital Infrastructure

OmnySports operates on a static-site architecture built using Astro and Bun and deployed via Cloudflare Pages infrastructure.

Because of this architecture:

* Users are not required to create accounts
* No login system exists
* No comment database is maintained
* No membership panel operates
* No internal user dashboard is provided

However, even static platforms process certain categories of technical data as part of normal internet functionality.

These processes occur at infrastructure and network levels.

Our content production standards are governed separately (on the phrase **content production standards** add link to Editorial Policy page).

---

# 2. Categories of Information Processed

Even without user registration systems, certain types of data may be processed.

## A. Technical & Network Information

When a user visits OmnySports, systems may process:

* IP address
* Browser type
* Device information
* Operating system
* Access time
* Referring source
* Network diagnostics
* Security flags

This information supports:

* Platform stability
* Performance optimization
* Abuse detection
* Threat mitigation
* Load balancing
* Security monitoring

This data is processed at infrastructure level and is not used for public profiling.

---

## B. Analytics & Audience Monitoring

OmnySports uses analytics, behavioral evaluation systems, and performance measurement technologies to understand how users interact with our platform.

These systems analyze:

* Engagement duration
* Scroll behavior
* Page navigation patterns
* Referral pathways
* General audience interest trends

This monitoring helps improve editorial depth, layout clarity, and coverage relevance.

Audience analysis does not result in public disclosure of identifiable personal profiles.

---

## C. Advertising Technology Processing

OmnySports displays advertisements through Raptive and its associated advertising ecosystem.

Advertising partners may process:

* Device identifiers
* Cookie-based identifiers
* IP address
* Generalized location information
* Engagement signals

Advertising operations are structurally separated from editorial governance as defined in our **advertising compliance framework** (on the phrase **advertising compliance framework** add link to Advertising Policy page).

We do not sell personal information.
