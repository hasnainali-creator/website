# Privacy Policy 

## Your Data. Your Rights. Our Responsibility.

At OmnySports, your privacy is not a legal checkbox. It is a commitment we uphold with the same discipline we apply to every piece of journalism we publish. We collect only what we need. We protect what we collect. And we are completely transparent about all of it.

This Privacy Policy explains — in plain, clear English — exactly what data we collect when you visit OmnySports, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you hold over it at all times. If you have ever wondered what happens to your information when you visit a sports news platform, this page answers every question. No legal fog. No buried clauses. Just honesty.

> *"Privacy is not a feature we bolt onto our platform. It is foundational to the trust our readers place in us — and we protect it accordingly."* — **OmnySports Editorial Board**

---

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Privacy protection is not isolated from our broader values. It operates within the same framework of trust and accountability that defines our journalism. As outlined in our [About Us](/about) page, OmnySports was built on a single promise: to serve our readers, not exploit them.

That promise extends directly to how we handle your personal data. We operate in full compliance with applicable global data protection regulations — including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for users in California, and equivalent frameworks in other jurisdictions where our readers are located.

**If it is not necessary, we do not collect it.** **If we collect it, we protect it.** **If you want it removed, we will remove it.**

---

## What Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with OmnySports voluntarily — by contacting us, subscribing to updates, or submitting content — we may collect the following:

*   **Full name** — when you register or contact us via our [Contact Us](/contact) page
*   **Email address** — for newsletters, alerts, or direct correspondence
*   **Message content** — when you submit feedback, corrections, or enquiries
*   **Account credentials** — if you create a registered user account on our platform

We do not ask for information we do not need. If a field on a form is marked optional, it genuinely is.

---

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit OmnySports, certain technical data is collected automatically by our servers and analytics systems. This includes:

*   **IP address** — used to determine approximate geographic location for content regionalisation
*   **Browser type and version** — to optimise page rendering and performance
*   **Device type** — desktop, mobile, or tablet, used for responsive design improvements
*   **Pages visited and time spent** — to understand which content serves our readers best
*   **Referring URL** — the page or platform that directed you to OmnySports
*   **Session duration and click patterns** — used for aggregate performance analytics only

This data is collected in anonymised or pseudonymised form wherever technically possible. It is never used to build individual user profiles for advertising purposes.

---

### Information Collected via Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform. A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your browser or device when you visit. It allows the site to remember certain information about your visit.

**How cookies work on OmnySports:**

1.  You arrive on our platform for the first time
2.  Our consent management system presents you with a clear cookie preference panel
3.  You choose which categories of cookies to accept or decline
4.  Your preferences are stored and respected for every future visit

The specific cookies we use, their purpose, and your full control options are explained in exhaustive detail on our [Cookie Policy](/cookie-policy) page. We strongly encourage you to read it.

## How We Use Your Information

Every piece of data we collect serves a specific, documented purpose. We do not process data speculatively or store it indefinitely "just in case."

| Data Type         | Purpose                                 | Legal Basis           |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Email address     | Newsletter delivery, account alerts     | Consent               |
| IP address        | Geographic content delivery, security   | Legitimate interest   |
| Analytics data    | Platform performance improvement        | Legitimate interest   |
| Contact form data | Responding to enquiries and corrections | Contractual necessity |
| Cookie data       | Session management, user preferences    | Consent               |

---

### How We Use Your Data

**Platform Performance:** We analyse aggregate, anonymised usage data to understand which content is most valuable to our readers, which pages load too slowly, and where user experience can be improved.

**Editorial Accountability:** When readers submit factual correction requests or contact our editorial team, we use the information provided solely to investigate and respond to that specific enquiry. This process is central to the accountability standards defined in our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy).

**Communications:** If you have subscribed to receive content alerts or newsletters, we use your email address to deliver that content. Every communication includes an unsubscribe option that works immediately and permanently.

**Legal Compliance:** In certain circumstances, we may be required to process or disclose data to comply with applicable law, regulatory requirement, or court order. We will always notify affected users where legally permissible to do so.

## What We Do Not Do With Your Data

This section is equally important. Here is what OmnySports categorically does not do:

*   **We do not sell your personal data.** Not to advertisers. Not to data brokers. Not to anyone.
*   **We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.**
*   **We do not build individual advertising profiles** based on your reading behaviour on our platform.

Our commercial operations — including advertising on our platform — are governed by our [Advertising Policy](/advertising-policy), which formally separates commercial relationships from editorial and reader data management.

## Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

OmnySports uses a small number of carefully selected third-party service providers to operate our platform effectively. These include:

**Analytics Providers:** We use industry-standard web analytics tools to measure aggregate platform performance. These tools receive anonymised, non-personally identifiable data only.

**Content Delivery Networks (CDNs):** To ensure fast page load speeds globally, we route content through CDN infrastructure. CDN providers process IP addresses solely for routing purposes and are contractually prohibited from using that data for any other purpose.

**Email Service Providers:** When we send newsletters or account-related communications, we use a third-party email platform. This platform processes your email address solely to deliver messages on our behalf. They do not have the right to use your email for their own marketing.

**Embedded Content:** Some articles on OmnySports may include embedded content from third-party platforms. When you interact with embedded content, the embedding platform may collect data about you according to their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing those policies independently.

---

### Data Processor Standards

Every third-party service provider we work with is subject to a formal Data Processing Agreement that requires them to:

*   Process your data only for the specific purpose we define
*   Maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures
*   Delete or return your data when the processing relationship ends
*   Never sell or share your data with additional parties without our explicit written consent

## Data Storage & Security

### Where Your Data Is Stored

OmnySports operates global infrastructure with data storage in secure, enterprise-grade facilities. We apply data residency principles wherever legally required — for example, ensuring that data from EU residents is processed and stored within the European Economic Area where applicable.

---

### How We Protect Your Data

We implement a layered security architecture to protect your personal information:

*   **Technical Safeguards:** Full SSL/TLS encryption across all pages and data transmissions, encrypted storage for all personally identifiable information, access controls ensuring only authorised personnel can access user data, and regular automated security scanning and vulnerability assessments.
*   **Organisational Safeguards:** All staff with access to user data undergo mandatory data protection training, data access is granted on a strict need-to-know basis, internal data handling procedures are reviewed and updated regularly, and data breach response protocols are documented, tested, and ready.

If a data breach were to occur that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of it — as required under GDPR and equivalent regulations.

---

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data

We do not store personal data longer than necessary. Our retention periods are defined by purpose:

*   **Contact form submissions** — retained for 12 months, then permanently deleted
*   **Newsletter subscriber data** — retained for the duration of the subscription, plus 30 days after unsubscription
*   **Analytics data** — retained in anonymised aggregate form for up to 26 months
*   **Legal compliance records** — retained for the minimum period required by applicable law

When retention periods expire, data is deleted through secure, irreversible processes.

## Your Rights Over Your Personal Data

### You Are in Control

Regardless of where you are located, OmnySports respects your rights over your personal data. Depending on your jurisdiction, these rights include:

**Right to Access:** You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this within 30 days of a valid request.

**Right to Rectification:** If any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct it immediately.

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** You have the right to request that we delete all personal data we hold about you, subject to any legal retention obligations that may apply.

**Right to Restriction of Processing:** You have the right to request that we pause the processing of your personal data while a dispute or review is underway.

**Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**Right to Object:** You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interest as our legal basis.

**Right to Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on your consent — such as cookies or newsletter subscriptions — you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time, with immediate effect.

---

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, visit our [Contact Us](/contact) page and submit a formal data rights request. Please include:

1.  Your full name and email address associated with your data
2.  The specific right you wish to exercise
3.  Any relevant details that will help us locate your data efficiently

We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours and fulfil it within 30 days.
