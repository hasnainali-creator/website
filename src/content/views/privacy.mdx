# Privacy Policy

Welcome to OmnySports. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you, including:
*   Identity Data: Name and username if you subscribe to our newsletter.
*   Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location.
*   Usage Data: Information about how you use our website.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
*   Provide you with the latest sports news and updates.
*   Improve our website's performance and user experience.
*   Send you newsletters if you have opted in.

## 3. Data Security
We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

## 4. Third-Party Links
This website may include links to third-party websites such as social media platforms. Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites.
