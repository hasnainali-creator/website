# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

OmnySports Global Media Group ("OmnySports", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our global audience. This Privacy Policy outlines our protocols for the collection, processing, and safeguarding of personal data in accordance with international data protection regulations, including the GDPR and CCPA.

## 1. Information Governance
We collect data only when necessary to enhance the precision of our sports journalism. This includes:
*   **Institutional Identity Data:** Professional credentials or names provided during subscription to our briefings.
*   **Analytical Metadata:** IP addresses, browser specifications, and engagement metrics to optimize our proprietary delivery infrastructure.
*   **Preference Data:** User-selected interests in specific athletic disciplines or geographic regions.

## 2. Utilization of Data
Data collected by OmnySports is utilized exclusively to:
*   Deliver high-precision sports journalism and real-time athletic briefings.
*   Monitor and refine the performance of the OmnySports global network.
*   Ensure the integrity of our interactive platforms and comment ecosystems.

## 3. Data Integrity and Security
OmnySports employs enterprise-grade cryptographic protocols to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of user data. Our internal security audits ensure that our "Precision Media" infrastructure remains resilient against emerging cyber threats.

## 4. Third-Party Integration and External Links
Our platform may feature integrations with institutional partners or social media networks. OmnySports does not exercise control over the privacy frameworks of these external entities. We advise users to review the privacy protocols of any third-party service accessed via our network.

## 5. Global Rights and Compliance
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. OmnySports honors all valid data sovereignty requests in compliance with applicable local and international laws.
