# 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access a website. They help websites function efficiently and provide insights into how visitors interact with content.

OmnySports uses cookies in a limited and responsible manner consistent with the operational standards described on our\
**About page** → (Link this to /about/)

# 2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for essential operational purposes only.

They help us:

* Maintain website functionality
* Improve site performance
* Analyze visitor interaction
* Support advertising systems
* Enable affiliate tracking transparency
* Enhance accessibility performance

Our editorial decisions are not influenced by cookies or advertising technologies. Editorial independence is governed by our\
**Editorial Policy** → (Link this to /editorial-policy/)

# 3. Types of Cookies Used

### A. Essential Cookies

These are required for:

* Basic navigation
* Secure access
* Page stability
* Technical performance

Without these cookies, the website may not function correctly.

---

### B. Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies collect aggregated, non-sensitive information such as:

* Page views
* Session duration
* Device types
* General traffic flow

This data helps improve user experience and content presentation.

---

### C. Advertising Cookies

OmnySports displays advertisements through Raptive and related partners.

Advertising cookies may:

* Deliver relevant ads
* Limit repetitive ads
* Measure advertising performance

Advertising structure and sponsorship transparency are explained in our\
**Advertising Policy** → (Link this to /advertising-policy/)

We do not manually control each automated advertisement.

**Advertising Policy** → (Link this to /advertising-policy/)
