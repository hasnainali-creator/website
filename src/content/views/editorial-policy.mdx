# Editorial Policy

At OmnySports, we are committed to providing the highest standards of sports journalism. Our editorial policy ensures that our content is accurate, fair, and independent.

## 1. Accuracy and Verification
We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. Our journalists are required to verify information with multiple sources whenever possible. In the event of an error, we are committed to correcting it promptly and transparently.

## 2. Independence and Impartiality
OmnySports maintains strict editorial independence. Our coverage is not influenced by advertisers, partners, or outside interests. We aim to provide impartial analysis and commentary on all sporting events.

## 3. Sourcing
We credit original sources of information and respect intellectual property rights. Anonymous sources are used only when necessary and after careful consideration of their reliability.

## 4. Diversity and Inclusion
We aim to cover a wide range of sports and athletes from diverse backgrounds, ensuring that our content reflects the global nature of the sports community.
