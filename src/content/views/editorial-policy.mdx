# Editorial Policy – OmnySports

At OmnySports, editorial responsibility is not a section of our website — it is the foundation of our entire operation.

This Editorial Policy defines how content is created, verified, reviewed, approved, updated, and protected before it reaches the public. It reflects our internal systems, professional standards, technological infrastructure, and long-term commitment to accuracy and accountability.

Our identity, mission, and structural background are explained separately (on the word **identity** add link to About page), but this page focuses exclusively on how we publish and maintain content standards.
---

# 1. Scope of Coverage

OmnySports publishes every type of sports-related content, including but not limited to:

* Breaking News
* Match Reports
* Tournament Coverage
* Statistical Reports
* Analytical Features
* Investigative Sports Developments
* Editorial Opinions
* Long-form Insights
* Sponsored Sports Features (when applicable)

All content is strictly limited to sports.\
We do not publish political, religious, entertainment, or unrelated trending material.

Sports journalism remains our sole editorial focus.

---

# 2. Source Verification Standards

Accuracy begins with sourcing.

OmnySports uses **only official and trusted sources**. These include:

* Official league communications
* Verified organizational announcements
* Direct statements from recognized authorities
* Official databases and governing bodies
* Authenticated press releases

We do not rely on unverified rumors.\
We do not publish speculative leaks.\
We do not cite anonymous sources.

If information cannot be confirmed through official channels, it is not published.

Our broader verification structure is aligned with the standards described in our **editorial governance framework** (on the phrase **editorial governance framework** add link to Terms page).

---

# 3. Multi-Layer Verification Infrastructure

OmnySports operates a dual-layer verification system:

## Layer 1: AI-Driven Verification Engine

Before human review begins, content is processed through advanced AI-based agents that:

* Cross-analyze real-time publicly available data
* Compare multiple official sources simultaneously
* Detect factual inconsistencies
* Validate statistical references
* Flag structural discrepancies
* Monitor alignment with verified databases

These AI systems operate in parallel, not sequentially.\
They compare datasets simultaneously to reduce margin of error.

However:

AI does not replace human editorial authority.\
It functions as a verification layer — not a publishing authority.

---

## Layer 2: Human Editorial Board Approval

After AI verification:

* Five highly experienced editorial professionals manually review the content.
* Each reviewer independently evaluates factual accuracy, clarity, structure, and compliance.
* If even a minor discrepancy is detected, the article is returned for revision.
* Publication occurs only after unanimous approval.

This review structure reflects the accountability standards described on our **professional contributors page** (on the phrase **professional contributors page** add link to Authors page).

There is no single-person approval model.\
Editorial decisions are structured and collective.

---

# 4. Publishing Protocol

Publishing speed never overrides accuracy.

### If information is fully verified:

We publish promptly.

### If information is partially confirmed:

We do not publish.

### If an urgent public development requires early reporting:

We clearly label the status of verification and include transparent notices explaining the stage of confirmation.

We never imply certainty where certainty does not exist.

---

# 5. Headline Integrity Policy

Headlines must:

* Accurately reflect the article
* Avoid exaggeration
* Avoid emotional manipulation
* Avoid misleading phrasing
* Avoid artificial urgency

Engagement is welcome.\
Misrepresentation is not.

Headline standards are reviewed as part of our internal compliance structure (on the word **compliance structure** add link to Disclaimer page).

---

# 6. News vs Opinion Distinction

OmnySports clearly differentiates between:

## News Reporting

Fact-based coverage using verified official information.\
No personal interpretation.

## Opinion / Editorial Content

Professional analysis, interpretation, or strategic breakdown written by qualified contributors.

Opinion content is:

* Clearly labeled
* Separated structurally
* Presented transparently

This separation protects reader clarity and editorial integrity.

---

# 7. Corrections & Update Transparency

Transparency is mandatory.

If:

* A factual error is identified
* A statistic changes
* A clarification becomes necessary
* Even a minor punctuation update occurs

We update the article and clearly mention:

* The date of update
* The nature of the update
* What was corrected or modified

We do not silently edit content.

Our update transparency reflects our public communication commitment (on the phrase **public communication commitment** add link to Contact page).

---

# 8. Archival Integrity

OmnySports does not routinely delete published content.

Instead:

* Articles are updated when necessary
* Context is added where appropriate
* Historical accuracy is preserved

Content removal is considered only under exceptional structural or legal necessity.

This approach aligns with our data retention standards (on the phrase **data retention standards** add link to Privacy page).

---

# 9. Plagiarism & Originality Policy

Zero tolerance.

All published material must be:

* Original
* Independently structured
* Properly attributed where required

Plagiarism detection tools are integrated into our review process.\
Violation of originality standards results in immediate internal review and corrective action.

Our intellectual property safeguards are further detailed within our **content protection policy** (on the phrase **content protection policy** add link to DMCA page).

---

# 10. Data Usage Standards

OmnySports uses:

* Official league statistics
* Verified governing body databases
* Confirmed performance metrics

We do not rely on speculative analytics or unofficial third-party projections.

Statistical integrity is considered part of our technical transparency obligations (on the phrase **technical transparency obligations** add link to Cookie Policy page).

---

# 11. Conflict of Interest Policy

If:

* OmnySports enters into a sponsorship agreement
* A contributor has a professional relationship with a covered entity

It must be clearly disclosed.

Sponsored content, if published, will:

* Be clearly labeled
* Remain factually detailed
* Maintain structural transparency
* Avoid misleading influence

Advertising relationships are kept separate from editorial control as described in our **advertising standards** (on the phrase **advertising standards** add link to Advertising Policy page).

Editorial decisions are never sold.

---

# 12. Legal & Ethical Safeguards

OmnySports does not publish:

* Defamatory content
* Misleading narratives
* Harmful misinformation
* Unverified allegations

Every article must align with ethical publishing principles and applicable regulations.

User rights and operational conditions are defined within our platform usage standards (on the phrase **platform usage standards** add link to Terms page).

We openly disclose:

AI systems assist in verification, data comparison, and structural review.

However:

* Final authority is human.
* Editorial decisions are human.
* Interpretation is human.
* Accountability is human.

AI enhances precision.\
It does not replace responsibility.
